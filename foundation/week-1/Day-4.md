# 1. WHAT IS ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE (AI)?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the ability of machines to simulate human intelligence such as learning, reasoning, problem-solving, and decision-making.

### Simple Definition

> AI enables machines to think, learn, and make decisions similar to humans.

---

## Goals of AI

✔ Learn from Data

✔ Understand Language

✔ Recognize Images

✔ Make Decisions

✔ Automate Tasks

---

## Major Branches

### 1. Machine Learning (ML)

Systems learn from data and improve automatically.

### 2. Deep Learning (DL)

Uses neural networks to solve complex problems.

### 3. Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Helps computers understand human language.

Examples:

* ChatGPT
* Google Translate

### 4. Computer Vision

Enables machines to understand images and videos.

Examples:

* Face Recognition
* Object Detection

### 5. Robotics

Combines AI with machines to perform tasks automatically.

### 6. Expert Systems

Rule-based systems that mimic human experts.

# 3. KEY AI TERMS

---

## 1. Model

A trained system that learns patterns from data and makes predictions.

### Example

A model trained to predict house prices.

---

## 2. Training Data

The dataset used to teach a model.

### Example

Historical sales data used for forecasting.

---

## 3. Parameters

Internal values learned during training that help the model make predictions.

### Example

Weights in a neural network.

---

## 4. Inference

The process of using a trained model to make predictions on new data.

### Example

Predicting whether a customer will buy a product.

---

## 5. Hallucination

When an AI system generates incorrect, misleading, or fabricated information while presenting it as factual.

### Example

AI inventing a source or giving incorrect statistics.

# 5. GAN (GENERATIVE ADVERSARIAL NETWORK)

## What is GAN?

A GAN is a deep learning model used to generate realistic images, videos, audio, and synthetic data.

---

## How GAN Works

Two neural networks compete against each other.

```text
Generator
    ↓
Creates Fake Data
    ↓
Discriminator
    ↓
Checks Real or Fake
    ↓
Feedback
    ↓
Generator Improves
```

---

## Applications of GAN

✔ AI Art Generation

✔ Image Enhancement

✔ Face Generation

✔ Deepfake Creation

✔ Data Augmentation
